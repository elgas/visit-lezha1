Lezha
See(current) Do Eat Guide

Welcome to Lezha

Your ideal destination for exploring untouched beaches, ancient heritage, delicious food and delightful summer days


Sights to see

Sandy beaches

Enjoy the most captivating sandy beaches. Everything is natural and you can explore new beaches yourself along the 20 kilometer coast line. The spot not to miss is called ‘Rana e Hedhun’, since it is still a virgin beach you can get to it only by walking.

4th century Ruins

Surrounding the National Hero's grave are the ruins of the earliest of the fortification walls of the city are of typical Illyrian construction and are dated to the late 4th century BC. The city was one of the main centres of the Illyrian kingdom.

Source Wikipedia

Lezha Castle

The castle bears traces of Illyrian, Roman, Byzantine and Ottoman architecture. Interesting places to visit are the ruins of the Ottoman buildings inside the castle, the mosque, the tower of the south-eastern wall with a Roman arch, and the Illyrian tower on the southern wall.

Source Wikipedia

Things to do

Hike the sandy mountain

The most fun part of the coast, is this big sandy mountain, which you get by the seaside, and you can take your time to hike up the hill. Make sure to have your camera with you to record the breathtaking view of the adriatic sea from the top of the mountain.

Take a boat tour

One of the best ways to enjoy the rich coast line. Lezha has it all, from the Kune-Vain-Tale Lagoon, a protected area in Albania, to beautiful unexplored beaches that all waiting for you to discover.

Explore the ancient history

Take the day to visit the historical cites. Start from the Illyrian ruins located in the city center and follow it by a short walk up to the castle. The castle offers a beautiful view of the Lezha fields and the Adriatic Sea.

Food you must try

Eat at the best restaurant in Albania

Mrizi i Zanave it is a world famous restaurant 10 minutes from the center of Lezha. It specializes in bringing traditional Albanian dishes with a twist of contemporary. Located in the mountany countryside it's a good change from the heat of the seaside.

Fresh Sea Food

Enjoy the perks of getting a daily cout fish in any restaurant on the coast. You can find a mixture of traditional and more daring chefs that will leave you coming back for more. Perfect date night where you can enjoy the sun setting in the sea makes a perfect romantic dinner.
